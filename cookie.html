Cookie Policy

Understanding how we use cookies to enhance your experience on ArtSpark

Last updated: January 30, 2025

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit our website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and to provide information to website owners.

At ArtSpark, we use cookies to enhance your browsing experience, remember your preferences, and provide personalized content that matches your interests in art and creativity.

Types of Cookies We Use

Cookie Type Purpose Duration
Essential Cookies Required for basic website functionality, navigation, and security Session/Persistent
Performance Cookies Help us understand how visitors interact with our website Up to 2 years
Functionality Cookies Remember your preferences and personalize your experience Up to 1 year
Marketing Cookies Used to deliver relevant advertisements and track ad effectiveness Up to 13 months

How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for various purposes to improve your experience on ArtSpark:

  • Authentication: To keep you logged in during your session and remember your login preferences
  • Personalization: To remember your favorite art categories, preferred artists, and customized settings
  • Analytics: To understand how you navigate our site and which artworks and blog posts are most popular
  • Security: To protect against fraud and ensure the security of your account and data
  • Content Delivery: To optimize loading times and deliver content efficiently
  • Marketing: To show you relevant art exhibitions, workshops, and artist features that match your interests

Third-Party Cookies

We may also use third-party cookies from trusted partners to enhance your experience:

  • Google Analytics: To analyze website traffic and user behavior
  • Social Media Platforms: For social sharing buttons and embedded content from Instagram, Facebook, and other platforms
  • Payment Processors: To securely process transactions for art purchases and commissions
  • Content Delivery Networks: To ensure fast loading of images and media content

Note: These third parties may use cookies according to their own privacy policies, which we encourage you to review.

Managing Your Cookie Preferences

You have several options to manage cookies:

Browser Settings

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can:

  • View and delete cookies that are already stored on your device
  • Block all cookies from being stored
  • Allow cookies only from specific websites
  • Block third-party cookies while allowing first-party cookies

Cookie Consent Banner

When you first visit ArtSpark, you'll see a cookie consent banner where you can:

  • Accept all cookies for the full website experience
  • Customize your preferences by cookie category
  • Accept only essential cookies

Important: Blocking certain cookies may limit your ability to use some features of our website, such as saving preferences, viewing personalized content, or completing purchases.

Cookie Retention

Different cookies have different lifespans:

  • Session Cookies: Deleted when you close your browser
  • Persistent Cookies: Remain on your device for a set period or until you delete them
  • Essential Cookies: Kept for as long as necessary for website functionality
  • Analytics Cookies: Typically retained for 24 months
  • Marketing Cookies: Usually expire after 13 months

We regularly review our cookie usage and retention periods to ensure we only keep data for as long as necessary.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in technology, legal requirements, or our practices. When we make significant changes, we will:

  • Update the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Notify you through a prominent notice on our website
  • Send you an email notification if you have an account with us
  • Request renewed consent if required by law

We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we use cookies.
