Protecting artistic intellectual property and respecting creators' rights on ArtSpark

Last updated: January 30, 2025

Copyright Protection

ArtSpark is committed to protecting the intellectual property rights of artists, creators, and content owners. All original artwork, photographs, written content, designs, and creative materials displayed on our platform are protected by copyright law and other applicable intellectual property rights.

Important: Unauthorized use, reproduction, distribution, or modification of copyrighted content without explicit permission from the copyright owner is strictly prohibited and may result in legal action.

By using ArtSpark, you acknowledge and agree to respect all copyright and intellectual property rights of the content creators and owners whose work appears on our platform.

ArtSpark Platform Content

Website Design and Code

The ArtSpark website design, layout, graphics, user interface, software code, and all related proprietary materials are owned by ArtSpark and are protected by copyright, trademark, and other intellectual property laws. This includes but is not limited to:

  • Website layout and visual design elements
  • Logo, branding, and trademark materials
  • Software code and functionality
  • Database structure and organization
  • Original written content and blog posts

User-Generated Content

Artists and users who upload content to ArtSpark retain full copyright ownership of their original works. By uploading content, users grant ArtSpark a limited, non-exclusive license to display, distribute, and promote their work on our platform for the purpose of operating our service.

Permitted Uses

The following uses are generally permitted under fair use or with proper attribution:

Personal Viewing

You may view and browse artwork on ArtSpark for personal, non-commercial purposes.

Social Sharing

You may share links to artwork pages on social media platforms, provided you credit the original artist.

Educational Use

Limited use for educational purposes with proper attribution to the artist and ArtSpark platform.

Press Coverage

Media outlets may use images for legitimate news coverage with proper attribution and artist credit.

Prohibited Uses

The following activities are strictly prohibited without explicit written permission from the copyright owner:

  • Commercial Use: Using artwork for commercial purposes, advertising, or profit-making activities
  • Reproduction: Copying, downloading, or reproducing artwork in any form
  • Distribution: Redistributing or selling artwork through other platforms or channels
  • Modification: Altering, editing, or creating derivative works based on original artwork
  • Unauthorized Display: Displaying artwork on other websites, publications, or physical locations
  • Plagiarism: Claiming artwork as your own or removing artist attribution
  • Bulk Download: Using automated tools to download multiple artworks

Warning: Violation of these terms may result in account suspension, legal action, and liability for damages.

DMCA Compliance

ArtSpark complies with the Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA) and responds promptly to valid copyright infringement notices. If you believe your copyrighted work has been used on our platform without authorization, you may submit a DMCA takedown request.

To file a DMCA takedown notice, please provide:

  1. Identification of the copyrighted work that you claim has been infringed
  2. Identification of the infringing material and its location on our website
  3. Your contact information including name, address, phone number, and email
  4. A statement of good faith belief that the use is not authorized by the copyright owner
  5. A statement of accuracy under penalty of perjury that you are authorized to act on behalf of the copyright owner
  6. Your physical or electronic signature

Send DMCA notices to: dmca@artspark.com

We will process valid DMCA requests within 24-48 hours and notify all relevant parties of the action taken.

Artist Rights and Responsibilities

Artist Rights

  • Retain full copyright ownership of uploaded artwork
  • Control how their work is displayed and promoted
  • Request removal of their content at any time
  • Receive proper attribution for their work
  • Pursue legal action against unauthorized use

Artist Responsibilities

  • Only upload original work or content they have rights to use
  • Provide accurate information about their artwork
  • Respect other artists' copyrights and intellectual property
  • Promptly respond to legitimate copyright concerns
  • Follow ArtSpark's community guidelines and terms of service

Licensing and Permissions

If you wish to use artwork from ArtSpark for purposes beyond what is permitted under fair use, you must obtain proper licensing or permission from the copyright owner. ArtSpark can help facilitate contact between interested parties and artists for licensing discussions.

Commercial Licensing

Contact artists directly for commercial use rights, reproduction licenses, or commission work.

Print Rights

Separate licensing may be required for print reproduction, merchandise, or physical display rights.

Artists may choose to offer their work under various licensing terms, including Creative Commons licenses, royalty-free use, or custom licensing agreements. Always verify the specific license terms before using any artwork.
